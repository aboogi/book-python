Syntax Comparison Operators
===========================


Less Than and Less or Equal Then
--------------------------------
* ``x < y`` - Less than
* ``x <= y`` - Less or equal

>>> 10 < 2
False

>>> 10 <= 2
False

>>> x = 10
>>> y = 2
>>>
>>> x <= 2
False


Greater Than and Greater or Equal Than
--------------------------------------
* ``x > y`` - Greater than
* ``x >= y`` - Greater or equal

>>> 10 > 2
True

>>> 10 >= 2
True

>>> x = 10
>>> y = 2
>>>
>>> x >= 2
True


Equals
------
* ``obj == obj``

>>> 10 == 2
False

>>> 0 == -0
True


Not Equals
----------
* Inversion of ``==``
* ``obj != obj``

>>> 10 != 2
True
